PremKing

A mobile application to determine your friendgroup's best guesser

Here's the Privacy Policy


In PremKing, you create or join a league with your friends, and throughout the Premier League's season, guess the fixture results. The player with the most correct guesses is awarded with the most points and will win the league by the season's end!
